About Me

Hello, my name is James. I am a software engineer with a background in applied math and computer science. I overcame many challenges along my journey in college and in App Academy. This journey shaped my resilience and will to never give up, setting the tone for my path into software engineering. The switch happened when I recognized the field's demand for consistent organization and preparation, which aligns with my values for work ethic. Beyond tech, my interest lies in self-growth, which seamlessly compliments my professional career, creating a feedback relationship that enriches both my work and life.
